Acromioclavicular Joint Dislocation

Anatomy / Upper Limb / Pectoral Region

Last Updated: 24th January 2019

Dislocation of the acromioclavicular joint may result from a direct blow to the joint, a hard fall onto the shoulder or from a fall onto an outstretched upper limb. In acromioclavicular joint dislocation the acromion is forcibly pushed inferiorly and medially with respect to the clavicle, and becomes more prominent.
